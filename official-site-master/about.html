A Brief Introduction

SEDS Antariksh is the one and only space club in VIT, Chennai with a motivated spirit to reconnect youth to the void out there, from where everything springs. We strive to provide opportunities to students who desire a career in astronomy aeronautics and astronautics, through a path of worthwhile learning with practical ends, with the help of eminent professionals in the know.

Life on Earth has come a long way... Beginning from an unintelligent promordial soup to culminate in the present-- in a species that is able to cognise its place in the grandness of existence, and still having a long, long way to go. We the youth at SEDS Antariksh VIT, Chennai Chapter, have initiated lift-off, soaring up in this way to limitless possibilities, to try and fathom the workings of the universe for the sake and good of all Earthlings, and of course, to re-connect youth to the void out there, from where everything springs!

What we have done

Our Achievements

MAY 2021 to present

The Space Science Challenge 2021 has been initiated by Spaceonova with an objective to induce problem-solving skills, design thinking and a solution-oriented mindset among students in the fields of space technology and exploration. This challenge has been divided into two categories - namely, Rocketry and Satellites. We have qualified the 1st round of both Rocketry and Satellite category till now as this event is ongoing.

MEMBERS PARTICIPATED:
Achyut Agrawal(team leader), Samridhi, Ojaswa Yadav, Kinshuk Dua

MARCH 2021 to APRIL 2021

Our two teams (Team Antariksh and Team Paradox) participated in International Astronomical Search collaboration which was conducted by NASA along with Hardin-Simmons University, SEDS India, Pan-Starrs and Catalina Sky Survey. Each team was successful to find 3 objects which gained preliminary status.

MEMBERS PARTICIPATED:
1) Team Antariksh- Ratnadeep Das Choudhury, Mangolik Kundu, Souvik Datta, Soham Bhattacharya
2) Team Paradox: Achyut Agrawal, Samridhi, Nishant Chaturvedi
