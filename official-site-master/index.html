About Us

SEDS Antariksh is the one and only space club in VIT, Chennai with a motivated spirit to reconnect youth to the void out there, from where everything springs. We strive to provide opportunities to students who desire a career in astronomy, aeronautics and astronautics, through a path of worthwhile learning with practical ends, with the help of eminent professionals in the know.

Projects

Over here at the Project Department of SEDS Antariksh, we try to instil a sense of interest and excitement among students about space and astronomy. Through our various technical projects and events, we try to build a community of students who can cater to the futuristic needs of the space and aerospace industry.

Your love for innovation, creation and space is all that it takes to become a member of our community.

Outreach

The Outreach department is responsible for organizing sessions in various schools where the kids are taught about basics regarding space starting with why astronomy is important to us till the big bang theory. It covers all the topics which are taught keeping the age and previous knowledge of children in mind.
